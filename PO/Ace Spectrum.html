  • Asexual=
    Is an umbrella term for the difference in lack of romantic or sexual attraction. But in itself it would be a person who lacks sexual attraction, so small to none. But they could want a non sexual relationship.

  • Aromantic=
    An individual who has a lack of romantic attraction. So small or close to nothing. They could still have sexual attraction and have platonic relationships.

  • Demisexual=
    An individual who doesn't have sexual attrection towards anyone until they have a deep emotional connection or just really know eachother. The abbreviation of Demisexual is Demi
