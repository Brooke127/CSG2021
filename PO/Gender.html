  • Cisgender=
    An individual that identifies like the gender they were assigned at birth.
    AMAB is the abbreviation of Assigned Male At Birth and AFAB is
    the abbreviation of Assigned Female At Birth. Cisgender people are
    also not included in the LGBTQIA+ community. But someone could be
    cis and have a different sexuality then hetero ofcourse.
  • Genderfluid=
    A genderidentity that refers to a gender that differs from time to
    time. It refers to someone that doesn't have a fixed gender. How you
    feel depends per person, some feel very feminine, some more masculine,
    some feel like both genders at once and other feels feel like neither.
    But it varries per person and time.
  • Genderqueer=
    Is the umbrella term for people that feel that their gender is different
    from their gender assigned at birth. It is similar to Non binary
    and could be used for people that do drag. but also just for anyone
    that feels midway gender or 75% one gender.

    Is first of all also the umbrella term for people that feel that their
    gender is different from what they were assigned at birth, just like
    Genderqueer. There are many labels under the umbrella like Genderfluid.
    Not only is Non-Binary an umbrella term though, come some also just
